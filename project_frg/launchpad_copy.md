## What is FORGE?

FORGE (FRG) is an AI agent compute token issued through the HACD Stack. Each FRG token represents one prepaid AI agent task run — when the task executes, the token is permanently burned.

FORGE is not a speculative asset or a staking product. It is a compute credit with a traceable on-chain formation origin. Every FRG token in existence was formed through a specific HACD unit and a verified stack cost.

---

## Why HACD?

Compute credits that can be freely minted are structurally indistinguishable from promises.

FORGE uses HACD Stack to anchor the supply origin to real energy expenditure:

- **Every HACD was PoW-mined** — the mining cost is a verifiable, sunk input
- **Every lot paid 50 HAC** — the stack cost is paid on-chain and visible
- **Every formation record is permanent** — formed-at timestamps cannot be backdated
- **No secret pre-minting** — formation is transparent and on-chain

Bitcoin proved PoW for money. HACD brings PoW to assets.

## How Stack Works

FORGE tokens enter supply through HACD Stack formation:

| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Acquire a HACD unit (PoW-mined, unique 6-letter name) |
| 2 | Pay 50 HAC stack cost on the Hacash Launchpad |
| 3 | 10,000 FRG formed on-chain, credited to your wallet |

Each lot is independent. All 200 lots follow the same rules. There are no tiered or discounted lots.

## Formation Rules

- **Total lots**: 200
- **Units per lot**: 10,000 FRG
- **Stack cost per lot**: 50 HAC + network fee
- **Maximum supply**: 2,000,000 FRG
- **All lots equal**: Yes
- **Removal effect**: Removing the stack disables the formation record for that lot

Formation closes when all 200 HACD lots are stacked. No additional FRG can be formed after that point.

## FAQ

**Do I need HACD to participate?**
Yes. Each lot requires one PoW-mined HACD unit. You cannot participate without a HACD.

**Do I need HAC?**
Yes. 50 HAC is required as the stack cost per lot, plus a small network fee.

**What happens when I use a FRG token?**
It is burned permanently. Your balance decrements by 1 per task.

**Can more FRG be created after all 200 lots are stacked?**
No. The maximum supply is 2,000,000 FRG across 200 lots. No additional formation is possible after that.

**Is this an investment product?**
No. FRG is a compute credit formed through HACD Stack. No profit is guaranteed. No price floor exists. Token value is not assured.

**What is the stack cost used for?**
The 50 HAC stack cost is a formation input — it is the on-chain cost of forming a Stack lot. It does not represent a price relationship or redemption right.
