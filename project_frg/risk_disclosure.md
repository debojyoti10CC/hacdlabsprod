## General Risk Statement

FORGE (FRG) is a HACD Stack Asset formed through the Hacash protocol. Participation in HACD Stack formation involves significant risks. You should not participate unless you fully understand these risks and are prepared to accept potential total loss of any HAC or HACD you contribute.

This is **not a financial product**, **not an investment product**, and **not a guaranteed return instrument**.

---

## Formation Risks

**Stack cost is not a price floor**
The 50 HAC stack cost paid per lot is a formation input. It does not create, imply, or guarantee a price relationship between FRG and HAC. There is no redemption mechanism tying FRG value to the stack cost.

**HACD unit cost is not recoverable**
The HACD unit used in stack formation cannot be refunded through FRG. If you choose to unstack (remove the HACD from the formation), this may affect the formation record. Consult the Hacash Launchpad documentation for the exact removal behavior before acting.

**Formation records are permanent**
Once a lot is formed, the formation record is written on-chain and cannot be altered. This applies to errors as well as intentional formations.

---

## Utility Risks

**Utility is roadmap-dependent**
FRG tokens represent a future AI agent task access claim. The AI agent task infrastructure is under development. There is no guarantee that the utility will be delivered on any specific timeline, or at all.

**Tokens burn on use and are not replaced**
Each use permanently reduces your FRG balance by 1. Burned tokens cannot be recovered or reissued.

**No passive income or yield**
FRG tokens do not generate staking rewards, interest, dividends, or any form of passive return. Holding FRG does not entitle the holder to any income.

## Market Risks

**No guaranteed liquidity**
FRG tokens may have limited or no secondary market liquidity. The ability to sell or trade FRG tokens cannot be guaranteed.

**Price is not guaranteed**
FRG may trade at any price, including below formation cost or at zero. No party guarantees a market price for FRG.

**No listing guarantee**
No exchange listing is guaranteed or implied by this issuance.
